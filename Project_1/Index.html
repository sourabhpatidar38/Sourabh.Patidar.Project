About us

Why we're different

Are you ready to start your next multimedia project? We know that when the time comes, you need a partner that not only understands and cares about your needs, but has the in-house capabilities to efficiently complete your project.

Based in Indore, SP Multimedia is a full-service multimedia production company. Founded in 2021 by photographer Sourabh Patidar, SP Multimedia (formerly known as SP Photography) is one of the only studios in the city offering a complete range of integrated services including:

  • Photography
  • Retouching
  • Video and Television Production
  • Digital Media
  • Computer Generated Imagery (CGI)
  • Stock and Custom Room Sets
